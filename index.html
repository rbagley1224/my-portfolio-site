About Me

Hey, I'm Riley Bagley — a self-taught web developer passionate about building clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites. I started learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript out of curiosity, and it quickly turned into a full-blown passion. I love turning creative ideas into real projects that help people and businesses stand out online.

I've been building projects that show off both the technical side of coding and the design aspect — because a website shouldn't just function, it should feel great to use. Whether it's a portfolio site, small business landing page, or a creative concept, I enjoy the process from start to finish.

Outside of coding, I'm into photography, videography, and storytelling. I've spent time creating travel videos, working on short films, and capturing moments that inspire. I'm from a small town, and I carry that grounded mindset with me into everything I create — whether it's a personal project or work for a client.

Right now, I'm focused on sharpening my front-end skills with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and starting to integrate React into my workflow. I also enjoy learning about UI/UX design, accessibility, and animation to create smooth, engaging experiences for users.

If you're looking for someone who can bring creativity, attention to detail, and a strong work ethic to your project — let's connect. Whether you're a brand, a small business, or someone with a cool idea, I'd love to help bring your vision to life.
